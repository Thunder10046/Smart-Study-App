Product Details

The Panasonic AG-HPX600PJ is a professional shoulder-mounted camcorder with a 2/3-inch 2.2MP 3-MOS sensor and AVC-Intra recording capabilities. It features a 16-bit A/D converter, a 20-bit DSP, and a 14-bit digital processing. The camcorder is compatible with P2 cards, and is ideal for broadcast, sports, and documentary production.
