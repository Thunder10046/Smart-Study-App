Product Details

The Manfrotto 502AH is a professional video head with a flat base and a 15.4 lb load capacity. It features a fluid drag system, a counterbalance system, and a sliding quick release plate. The video head is compatible with a wide range of tripods and monopods, and is ideal for videographers who need a reliable and high-performance video head for their productions.
