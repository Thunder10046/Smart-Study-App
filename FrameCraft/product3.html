Product Details

The Canon EOS D60 is a professional-grade camera with a 20.1MP sensor and 4K video recording capabilities. It features a 3.2-inch LCD touchscreen, built-in Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity, and a 45-point all cross-type AF system. The camera is compatible with EF and EF-S lenses, and comes with an 18-135mm lens. The Canon EOS D60 is ideal for photographers and videographers who need a versatile and high-performance camera for a wide range of applications.
