About Us

We are ALDENAIRE Football Club, a group of passionate football enthusiasts who have come together to share their love for the beautiful game. Our members are:

Andy, Brian, Chris, Danny, David, James, John, Justin, Luke, Mark, Michael, Paul, and Ryan.

We started out as a small group of friends who would play football in a local park, but as time went on, we grew in numbers and became more than just a casual gathering.

We were officially registered in 2017 and began playing in local leagues, and since then, we have grown to become one of the most popular football clubs in the area.

We are proud of our humble beginnings and the hard work that has gone into building our club over the years. Come and join us, and be a part of our journey as we continue to grow and succeed together.
