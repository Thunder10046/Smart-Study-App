Club History

ALDENAIRE Football Club was established in 2015 by a group of friends who shared a passion for football.

The club started off as a small gathering of friends who played football in a local park, but as time went by, more and more people started to show interest in joining the club.

In 2017, the club was officially registered and began to play in local leagues. Since then, the club has grown significantly and has become one of the most popular football clubs in the area.
