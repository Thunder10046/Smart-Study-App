Luis Mendez is a talented defender of the ALDENAIRE Football club. He was born in the city of Ecuador on November 13th, 1996.

He started playing football at a young age and quickly showed his talent in the game. He is known for his leadership, physicality and his great ability to win headers and tackles.

He is currently studying Business Administration at the University of Guayaquil and is a regular member of the Ecuadorian National Team.
